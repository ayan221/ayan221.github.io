I work on computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on human motion generation, millimeter-wave human pose estimation, and humanoid robotics. My research aims to enable privacy-preserving human sensing and high-quality human motion data generation without relying on costly capture equipment. I am also a visiting researcher at Carnegie Mellon University (Oct. 2025–Oct. 2026), working on motion generation for humanoid robots.
